About us

In 2011 I had a dream of a place where all people can come to play and show their creative side, because deep down we all have one. The problem is, none of us have time and if we do… we might not have the money. That’s why I opened Creadores, to create a place where everyone can tap into their creative side, relax a bit and unwind. Even with a tight budget or schedule.

What we offer

Not only do we sell craft supplies, we also do laser cutting and engraving. Need something personal and special for an upcoming birthday? No stress, email or call us with your idea and we can make it a reality. Need a cake topper for your wedding? Piece of cake. With our team’s creativity and personal touch, anything is possible.
